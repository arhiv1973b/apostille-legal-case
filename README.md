## Summary

This repository contains a civil lawsuit against the Republic of Moldova for damages caused by:

- Systematic violation of the right to a fair trial (Art. 6 ECHR)
- Failure to investigate torture allegations (Art. 3 ECHR)
- Denial of effective remedy (Art. 13 ECHR)
- Illegal apostille certification of court decisions

## Evidence

- 90 apostilles with systematic violations
- 14.4% have "unclear signature"
- Repeat case numbers with different apostilles
- Pattern of procedural irregularities

## Legal Basis

1. European Convention on Human Rights (ECHR)
2. Civil Code of the Republic of Moldova
3. Hague Convention of 1961 (Apostille)
4. Rome Statute of the International Criminal Court
