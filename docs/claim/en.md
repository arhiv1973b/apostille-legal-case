### SUBJECT OF DISPUTE

The Plaintiff files a lawsuit against the State of the Republic of Moldova for compensation for material and moral damage caused as a result of:

1. Systematic violation of the right to a fair trial (Art. 6 ECHR)
2. Failure to investigate torture and cruel treatment allegations (Art. 3 ECHR)
3. Denial of effective remedy (Art. 13 ECHR)
4. Illegal apostille certification of court decisions without proper verification

The Plaintiff states that throughout the period **1997-2026** (29 years), systematic judicial persecution was carried out against him/her with gross violations of procedural norms.

As part of this case, **90 (ninety) apostilles** were issued for court decisions issued by various judicial instances of the Republic of Moldova during **2021-2023**.

According to the apostille registry (Apostille Archive):

- **13 apostilles** (14.4%) have "unclear signature"

- Inadequate verification of document authenticity
- Mass automatic legalization without verification

#### 3. Systemic Violations

The Plaintiff points to systemic violations:

- Repeated case numbers with different apostilles
- Lack of verification of court decision validity
- Ignoring torture allegations
- Systematic refusal to review cases
